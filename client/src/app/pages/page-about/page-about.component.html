About Pokémon of the Week

The Pokémon of the Week club is a group dedicated to the exploration and collection of creatures called Pokémon.

Each week, players will receive Poké Balls that they can use to find wild Pokémon to add to their collection.

Players may also search through the Teams pages of colleagues to find additional items and new Pokémon.

Visit the game guide to learn more.

Questions? Comments? Email {{contact.label}}.

Join the community in the Chat. Or join the Raids Chat.

You can also file bugs to the {{bugs.label}} . Major bugs, where sharing it publicly or not disclosing it would break the game in some way (quickly gaining/losing infinite resources, blocking other players from fair play, or another significant vulnerability ), can be responsibly disclosed via (1) email or chat with the game creator, or (2) through a bug. Rather than exploiting the bug, those that responsibly disclose it will receive a shiny Pokémon of your choice. It will be a var0, and can have a custom legal movepool of your choice if it has not been defined already.
